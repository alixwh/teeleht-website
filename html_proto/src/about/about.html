Our passion for tea started many years ago. In 1978 we made it our mission to provide the best tea. Since then we have delivered unique and authentic tea experiences to customers all over the world.

Our teas are freshly harvested from small farms in the mountains of Himalaya. We only select the finest leaves and hand craft them into the most flavorful blends.

We take pride in our commitment to sustainability and ethical sourcing. We work with suppliers who share our values and are committed to reducing their environmental footprint while supporting the livelihoods of the communities they work with.

At our tea shop, you'll find a wide range of teas to suit every taste and occasion. From classic blends like Earl Grey and English Breakfast to rare and exotic teas like matcha and pu-erh, we have something for everyone.

We also offer a variety of tea accessories, including teapots, cups, and infusers, to help you enjoy your tea to the fullest.

We believe that tea is not just a beverage, but a way of life, and we are here to share that with you. Whether you're a seasoned tea connoisseur or a first-time tea drinker, we invite you to come and explore our collection of teas and discover something new.
